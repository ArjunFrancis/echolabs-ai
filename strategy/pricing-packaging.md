## Product Packaging & Tiers

**Freemium Tier** (Lead Generation):
- **Features**: Basic LLM evaluation (5 models), compliance checklist, bootcamp access
- **Limits**: 100 evaluations/month, community support, watermarked reports
- **Price**: Free (credit card optional for advanced features)
- **Target**: Bootcamp participants, AI enthusiasts, small teams
- **Conversion Goal**: 25% upgrade to Pro within 90 days
- **Value**: AED 5k equivalent; builds pipeline, validates ICP

**Pro Tier** (Individual Teams):
- **Features**: Unlimited evaluations (20+ models), basic compliance scoring, API access, email support
- **Limits**: 5 users, 10 datasets, standard reports
- **Price**: AED 2,500/user/month (annual discount 15%) or AED 25k/year/user
- **Target**: AI/ML engineers, compliance analysts, mid-sized teams
- **Value**: AED 150k+ annual savings (manual audits, consultant fees)
- **Upsell Path**: Add consulting hours, advanced datasets

**Enterprise Tier** (Departmental):
- **Features**: All Pro + custom datasets, NDMO/DIFC compliance templates, priority support, SSO (Azure AD)
- **Limits**: 25 users, unlimited datasets, custom dashboards, audit logs
- **Price**: AED 120k/year base + AED 3k/user/month (min 10 users)
- **Target**: Financial institutions, government departments, hospitals
- **Value**: AED 1M+ savings (regulatory fines, compliance consultants)
- **Add-ons**: Sector-specific modules (AED 30k each)

**Custom Enterprise** (Organization-wide):
- **Features**: White-label platform, on-premises option, dedicated compliance officer, unlimited everything
- **Limits**: Custom SLAs (99.99% uptime), 24/7 support, co-development
- **Price**: AED 500k+ base; project-based consulting (AED 5k/day)
- **Target**: Large banks, federal ministries, healthcare networks
- **Value**: AED 5M+ ROI (enterprise-wide compliance, strategic partnership)
- **Contract**: 3-year minimum; volume discounts

**Consulting Packages** (Standalone/Bundled):
- **AI Readiness Audit**: AED 50k (5-day assessment, maturity report)
- **Compliance Implementation**: AED 150k (3-month project, NDMO certification)
- **Custom Evaluation Framework**: AED 200k (bespoke datasets, model fine-tuning)
- **Bootcamp + Platform**: AED 75k (training + 6-month Pro access)
- **Bundling**: 20% discount when combined with SaaS tiers
